## Engineering Approach

<table border="0">
<tr>
<td valign="middle" width="60%">

Good engineering is a product decision before it's a technical one.

Before writing a line of code, I ask: *what's the actual problem, who has it, and what's the simplest system that solves it without breaking at scale?*

I take ownership of the full delivery cycle — requirements, architecture, implementation, deployment, and iterating based on what real users actually do. I don't hand off problems. I solve them.
